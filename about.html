Ayaz Rashid is an emerging Architect & Entrepreneur in Pakistan. He is the Founder, Chief Architect & Managing Director of Hidden Lines Architects.

With his dynamic persona, Ayaz is redefining the eateries of Lahore. His body of work spans a broad range of scales and sectors, restaurants to urban, retail, commercial, residential & corporate.

Born in Kashmir Pakistan, Ayaz Rashid holds a Masters Degree in Interior Design (NCA), B.Arch (NCA) & B.Sc double Math-Physics.
